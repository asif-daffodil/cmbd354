Our Top Artist

Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo da Vinci was one of the exceptionally talented famous artists who showed a keen interest in many spheres of life outside of art. Yet, he often used his art to share these ideas and left behind a treasure trove of journals and artworks that provided us with considerable insight into the workings of one of the world’s most fascinating minds.
